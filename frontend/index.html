About Verta Project

Verta is an academic research project focused on developing accessible AI solutions for the Egyptian deaf community. Our technology leverages TensorFlow.js and custom datasets to accurately recognize and generate Arabic Sign Language.

Technical Implementation

  • TensorFlow.js for browser-based machine learning
  • Custom Arabic Sign Language dataset training
  • Real-time pose estimation and gesture classification
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Privacy-focused client-side processing
